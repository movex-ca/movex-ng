Movex respects user privacy and aims to collect only information reasonably necessary to operate, secure, improve, verify, and manage logistics services and related user accounts.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the account type and service use, Movex may collect:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, phone number, sex, address, and state.
  • Business or company information such as company name, registration details, contact details, and operating data.
  • Driver-related information such as vehicle type, pool preference, verification details, and operating affiliation details where applicable.
  • Order-related information such as pickup, drop-off, goods details, pool type, and service preferences.
  • Verification or approval data required for admin review, compliance, safety, or promotional decisions.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Support logistics requests, operational matching, and service coordination.
  • Verify users, drivers, businesses, companies, and selected platform relationships.
  • Manage approvals, promotions, service pools, and compliance checks.
  • Improve platform safety, support, reporting, and service delivery.
  • Respond to support issues, disputes, or operational incidents.

3. Information by User Category

Individual Customer / Sender

For customer accounts, Movex may collect registration information, order details, service preferences, and optional student-promo related documents where submitted.

Driver / Rider

For drivers and riders, Movex may collect registration information, preferred pool, vehicle details, affiliation information, and later verification or approval documents as required for activation.

Business Logistics Partner

For business partner accounts, Movex may collect business identity data, contact information, service categories, driver onboarding information, and operational records related to partner activities.

Company Needing Services

For company client accounts, Movex may collect company identity data, contact information, order data, billing-related information, and authorized-user management information where such features are enabled.

4. Information Sharing

Movex may share information only where reasonably necessary for platform operations, lawful compliance, order fulfillment, verification, support, insurance processing, commercial operations, or platform safety.

  • Information may be shared with operational partners or service providers where necessary for service delivery.
  • Information may be shared with insurers or verification-related providers where a service requires it.
  • Information may be disclosed where required by law, regulation, lawful request, or compliance obligation.

No mobile information will be shared or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Student Promo and Verification

Where a user uploads student identification for a possible promotion, the submitted information may be used only for verification, promo review, and admin decision-making.

  • Student-related promo approval is not automatic.
  • Movex may approve, reject, or request more information before any promo is applied.
  • Submission of student information does not guarantee a discount or promotional benefit.

6. Associations, Motor Garages, and Internal Tagging

Where Movex manages recognized associations or motor garage relationships, affiliation information may be stored, reviewed, or assigned internally by admin to support operations, reporting, and commission logic.

7. Data Security and Retention

  • Movex will take reasonable measures to protect stored user information.
  • Access may be restricted to authorized operational, technical, or administrative roles.
  • Information may be retained for operational, legal, compliance, audit, support, and service history purposes as reasonably necessary.

8. Cookies, Sessions, and Basic Technical Data

Movex may use cookies, session controls, and technical information necessary for login, account handling, performance, and secure platform access.

9. Changes to This Policy

Movex may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after updates means you accept the revised version.

10. Contact

For privacy, data, support, or compliance questions, users should contact the official Movex support details that will appear on the live platform.
